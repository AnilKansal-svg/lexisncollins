Chemical

India’s chemicals industry is one of the largest in the world, spanning petrochemicals, specialty chemicals, agrochemicals, polymers, dyes, and performance materials. With strong domestic demand, global supply chain realignments, and government initiatives such as the Production Linked Incentive (PLI) scheme, the sector is poised for robust growth. However, the industry also operates under a stringent regulatory environment covering environmental standards, hazardous substances, worker safety, trade controls, and intellectual property rights.

Our Chemicals Practice brings together deep sector expertise and multidisciplinary legal capabilities to advise multinational corporations, domestic manufacturers, distributors, and investors. We assist clients across the value chain – from large integrated chemical producers to specialty chemical innovators – on regulatory, transactional, and dispute-related matters.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • Regulatory & Compliance: Advising on compliance with the Chemicals (Management and Safety) Rules, Environmental Protection Act, Hazardous Waste Rules, Manufacture, Storage and Import of Hazardous Chemicals Rules (MSIHC), and other central and state regulations.
  • ESG & Sustainability: Supporting clients in carbon reduction, green chemistry initiatives, ESG-linked financing, and compliance with climate change and sustainability frameworks.
  • Commercial & Supply Contracts: Drafting and negotiating agreements for raw material supply, distribution, technology licensing, toll manufacturing, and joint R&D collaborations.
  • Foreign Investment & Market Entry: Assisting global chemical majors with foreign direct investment (FDI), joint ventures, technology transfers, and structuring of Indian operations.
  • M&A and Strategic Transactions: Advising on mergers, acquisitions, divestments, and private equity investments in chemical companies, including due diligence and structuring.
  • Trade & Customs: Advising on import/export restrictions, anti-dumping investigations, customs classifications, and WTO compliance in the global chemicals trade.
  • Intellectual Property: Protecting proprietary formulations, patents, process technologies, and trademarks, including enforcement strategies for chemical innovations.
  • Health, Safety & Environmental Matters: Assisting with compliance on occupational health and safety standards, hazardous material handling, chemical accident rules, and liability under environmental law.
  • Dispute Resolution: Representing clients in commercial, contractual, regulatory, and environmental disputes before courts, tribunals, and arbitral forums.
  • Technology & Innovation: Advising on digital transformation in chemical manufacturing, AI-driven process optimization, and collaborations in sustainable and specialty chemicals.

Why Choose Us?

With India emerging as a global hub for specialty and performance chemicals, our Chemicals Practice is uniquely positioned to guide clients through regulatory challenges, strategic investments, and sustainability transitions. By integrating expertise from our environment, ESG, trade, competition, technology, and disputes practices, we deliver holistic legal solutions tailored to the needs of the chemical sector.
