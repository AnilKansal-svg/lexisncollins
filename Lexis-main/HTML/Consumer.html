Consumer Products

India’s consumer products sector is one of the fastest-growing in the world, driven by rising disposable incomes, urbanization, digital adoption, and a rapidly expanding middle class. Global and domestic companies are scaling their presence across fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG), retail, luxury, lifestyle, electronics, health & wellness, and e-commerce. However, the sector is governed by a complex legal landscape spanning consumer protection, product liability, advertising, labeling, environmental compliance, and digital commerce regulations.

Our Consumer Products Practice advises multinational corporations, domestic enterprises, start-ups, and investors on the full spectrum of legal and regulatory issues impacting the consumer goods value chain. We deliver sector-specific, business-focused advice to help clients build sustainable brands and navigate India’s evolving consumer markets.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • Regulatory & Compliance: Advising on laws governing product standards, labeling, advertising, packaging, and safety under the Consumer Protection Act, Legal Metrology Act, Drugs & Cosmetics Act, and Food Safety & Standards Act (FSSA).
  • Product Liability & Consumer Disputes: Representing clients in consumer forums, product recall matters, warranty claims, and liability disputes relating to defective goods.
  • Commercial & Distribution Contracts: Drafting and negotiating supply chain, distribution, dealership, retail, and franchising agreements.
  • Digital Commerce & Technology: Advising on e-commerce regulations, digital marketplaces, data protection, online advertising, influencer marketing, and consumer-facing technology platforms.
  • M&A and Strategic Transactions: Assisting in acquisitions, joint ventures, brand licensing, and private equity investments in consumer product companies.
  • Intellectual Property & Branding: Protecting trademarks, designs, copyrights, trade dress, and enforcement of brand rights across physical and digital markets.
  • Competition & Trade: Advising on pricing practices, restrictive trade arrangements, competition law risks, and cross-border trade and customs regulations.
  • ESG & Sustainability: Guiding clients on sustainable packaging, plastic waste management, green product labeling, and ESG-driven consumer disclosures.
  • Dispute Resolution: Representing clients in commercial disputes, contractual claims, regulatory actions, and arbitration involving consumer product businesses.

Why Choose Us?

With India’s consumer sector rapidly expanding and increasingly regulated, our practice provides end-to-end legal support for companies building resilient and compliant businesses. Leveraging expertise across retail, e-commerce, intellectual property, ESG, competition, and disputes, we are well-positioned to advise global and domestic brands in navigating India’s dynamic consumer landscape.
