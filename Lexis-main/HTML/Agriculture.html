Agriculture, Agrichemicals and Fertilizers

India’s agriculture sector remains the backbone of its economy, contributing significantly to GDP, employment, and global trade. With rapid technological transformation, increasing private sector participation, and evolving government policies, agribusiness has emerged as a high-growth industry. International investors, multinational corporations, and domestic stakeholders alike are exploring opportunities across food production, agritech, supply chain, agrichemicals, and fertilizers. At the same time, the sector is governed by a complex framework of regulations relating to land use, environmental standards, licensing, product approvals, trade restrictions, and intellectual property protection.

Our Agriculture, Agrichemicals and Fertilizers Practice is uniquely positioned to advise clients across the entire value chain – from seed producers, agrochemical manufacturers, and fertilizer companies, to large-scale agribusinesses, commodity traders, and investors. We combine sector-specific knowledge with strong regulatory, transactional, and dispute resolution capabilities to deliver commercially sound legal solutions.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • Regulatory & Compliance: Advising on laws and regulations governing seeds, pesticides, fertilizers, biotechnology, GMOs, food safety, and environmental standards, including compliance with the Fertiliser (Control) Order, Insecticides Act, and allied legislation.
  • Market Entry & Structuring: Assisting global agrichemical and fertilizer companies in setting up operations in India, including foreign direct investment (FDI), joint ventures, licensing, and distribution arrangements.
  • Commercial Contracts: Drafting and negotiating supply, distribution, dealership, and collaboration agreements tailored to the agriculture sector.
  • Environmental & Sustainability Advisory: Guiding clients on ESG considerations, sustainable farming practices, carbon credits, and climate-linked obligations impacting agricultural enterprises.
  • Trade & Competition: Advising on cross-border trade, import/export restrictions, WTO compliance, competition law issues, and anti-dumping disputes in agrichemicals and fertilizers.
  • Mergers & Acquisitions: Structuring and executing M&A transactions, strategic alliances, and private equity investments in agribusiness and input manufacturing sectors.
  • Intellectual Property: Protecting plant varieties, biotech innovations, proprietary formulations, trademarks, and patents in agrichemicals and fertilizers.
  • Dispute Resolution: Representing clients in sector-specific disputes, regulatory challenges, commercial arbitrations, and litigation across India.
