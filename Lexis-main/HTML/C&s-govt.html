Central and State Government Work Practice

Engaging with government at both the central and state levels is integral to doing business in India. From policy formulation to regulatory compliance, approvals, licensing, public procurement, and dispute resolution, government interaction shapes the operational landscape for companies across industries. India’s governance framework is complex, with central laws and state-specific regulations often intersecting, requiring careful navigation and strategic legal support.

Our Central and State Government Work Practice advises multinational corporations, domestic enterprises, investors, and industry associations in their dealings with various ministries, regulatory authorities, and state departments. We combine legal expertise with an understanding of administrative processes, enabling clients to effectively structure, negotiate, and secure clearances for projects and business operations in India.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • Regulatory & Policy Advisory: Advising on central and state-level laws, delegated legislations, and policy frameworks affecting diverse sectors including infrastructure, energy, technology, aviation, agriculture, and real estate.
  • Government Approvals & Licensing: Assisting clients in obtaining clearances, permits, industrial licenses, and registrations from central and state agencies, including environmental and sector-specific approvals.
  • Public Procurement & Tenders: Advising on government tenders, concessions, bidding strategies, PPP projects, and contract management with ministries and state authorities.
  • FDI & Market Entry: Supporting multinational corporations in structuring investments, joint ventures, and collaborations in compliance with central and state regulations.
  • Dispute Resolution: Representing clients in writ petitions, administrative challenges, and regulatory disputes before High Courts, the Supreme Court of India, and sectoral tribunals.
  • Policy Engagement & Advocacy: Assisting industry bodies and clients in policy submissions, stakeholder consultations, and legislative advocacy with government institutions.
  • Infrastructure & Project Development: Advising on concessions, land acquisition, state incentives, and central/state project clearances for large-scale infrastructure and industrial projects.
  • ESG & Compliance: Supporting businesses in aligning with government-driven ESG mandates, climate commitments, and sustainability-linked regulatory frameworks.
  • Inter-Governmental Matters: Advising on issues arising from federal distribution of powers, interstate commerce, taxation, and overlapping regulatory jurisdictions.

Why Choose Us?

With a strong understanding of India’s federal governance structure, our practice provides clients with the clarity and strategy required to operate in compliance with central and state requirements while advancing their business objectives. By leveraging our multidisciplinary expertise across infrastructure, ESG, competition, trade, technology, and disputes, we deliver comprehensive legal support at the intersection of business and government.
