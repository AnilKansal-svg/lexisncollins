E-commerce

India is one of the fastest-growing e-commerce markets globally, driven by rising internet penetration, digital payments, and a rapidly expanding consumer base. With increasing participation by global platforms, domestic players, start-ups, and investors, the sector has become central to retail, logistics, fintech, and digital innovation. However, the industry operates in a complex regulatory environment covering consumer protection, foreign investment, data privacy, competition law, and sector-specific restrictions on online trade.

Our E-Commerce Practice advises multinational platforms, digital marketplaces, technology companies, logistics providers, consumer brands, and investors across the e-commerce ecosystem. We deliver integrated legal solutions that address regulatory compliance, commercial strategy, dispute management, and cross-border structuring for businesses operating in India’s digital economy.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • Regulatory & Compliance: Advising on the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, Information Technology Act, intermediary liability, data protection, advertising standards, and emerging digital commerce regulations.
  • Foreign Direct Investment (FDI): Assisting global e-commerce companies on structuring marketplace models, compliance with Press Notes, and navigating restrictions under India’s FDI policy.
  • Commercial & Technology Contracts: Drafting and negotiating agreements for online marketplaces, logistics, cloud hosting, software licensing, payment gateways, influencer marketing, and third-party service providers.
  • Data Privacy & Cybersecurity: Advising on compliance with India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act, IT security obligations, and cross-border data transfers.
  • M&A and Strategic Transactions: Guiding clients on acquisitions, joint ventures, private equity investments, and strategic alliances in the e-commerce and digital retail space.
  • Competition & Antitrust: Advising on investigations, pricing practices, exclusive arrangements, and compliance with the Competition Act.
  • Consumer Protection & Liability: Representing clients in consumer disputes, product liability claims, unfair trade practice allegations, and regulatory investigations.
  • Intellectual Property & Branding: Protecting trademarks, copyrights, and brand identity in online platforms, including enforcement against counterfeiting and infringement.
  • Taxation & Customs: Advising on GST, customs duties, cross-border supply chains, and digital taxation of e-commerce operators.
  • ESG & Sustainability: Supporting clients in compliance with sustainable packaging, waste management, and responsible digital practices.

Why Choose Us?

With India’s e-commerce sector continuing to expand and diversify into areas such as quick commerce, D2C brands, and digital marketplaces, our practice provides end-to-end support to global and domestic players. By leveraging our expertise across technology, data privacy, consumer law, competition, and dispute resolution, we deliver comprehensive solutions for businesses navigating India’s evolving digital economy.
