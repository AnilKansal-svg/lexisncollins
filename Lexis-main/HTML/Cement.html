Cement

The cement industry is a cornerstone of India’s infrastructure and real estate development, driving growth in housing, industrial construction, and large-scale infrastructure projects. As one of the largest producers and consumers of cement globally, India offers significant opportunities for domestic and international players in manufacturing, supply chain, technology, and green cement innovation. The sector, however, is highly regulated, energy-intensive, and subject to scrutiny under environmental, competition, and trade laws, making legal expertise essential for successful operations.

Our Cement Practice brings together deep industry knowledge and multi-disciplinary legal capabilities to advise cement manufacturers, equipment suppliers, investors, real estate developers, contractors, and multinational corporations. We support clients on regulatory compliance, complex transactions, sustainability initiatives, and sector-specific disputes.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • Regulatory & Compliance: Advising on the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act, environmental clearances, pollution control norms, and state-specific industrial policies impacting cement production and distribution.
  • M&A and Investments: Structuring and executing mergers, acquisitions, joint ventures, private equity, and foreign direct investment in cement companies and allied businesses.
  • Commercial & Supply Contracts: Drafting and negotiating contracts for raw material supply (limestone, coal, fly ash), equipment procurement, EPC agreements, and distribution arrangements.
  • Infrastructure & Project Development: Supporting cement manufacturers and investors in setting up new plants, expansion projects, and integrated facilities, including land acquisition, power supply, and logistics
  • ESG & Sustainability: Advising on decarbonization, energy efficiency, green cement, waste-to-energy projects, carbon credits, and ESG-linked financing.
  • Competition & Antitrust: Representing clients in cartel investigations, pricing disputes, and compliance with the Competition Act.
  • Trade & Customs: Advising on import/export regulations, anti-dumping investigations, and WTO compliance for cement and clinker trade.
  • Dispute Resolution: Representing clients in commercial disputes, construction arbitration, supply chain litigation, and regulatory challenges.
  • Technology & Innovation: Assisting on IP protection, licensing, and collaborations related to green cement technologies, AI-driven manufacturing, and digital supply chain solutions.

Why Choose Us?

With India’s ambitious infrastructure pipeline and focus on sustainable construction, our Cement Practice is strategically positioned to advise global and domestic stakeholders in navigating opportunities and challenges in this vital sector. We collaborate seamlessly with our infrastructure, real estate, ESG, energy, mining, and disputes practices to deliver comprehensive, sector-specific legal solutions.
