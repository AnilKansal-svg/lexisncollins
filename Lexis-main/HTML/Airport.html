Airports and Aviation Practice

India is one of the fastest-growing aviation markets in the world, with exponential growth in air traffic, increased private participation, and major investments in airport infrastructure. The sector is shaped by evolving regulatory frameworks, ambitious government initiatives such as UDAN (Regional Connectivity Scheme), and significant foreign investment in airlines, MROs, cargo, and airport operations. These developments present immense opportunities, but also complex legal and regulatory challenges for stakeholders across the aviation ecosystem.

Our Airports and Aviation Practice advises a wide range of clients including airlines, airport operators, MRO providers, lessors, financers, cargo companies, aerospace manufacturers, and investors. With deep industry knowledge and cross-border expertise, we support clients on transactions, regulatory compliance, financing, disputes, and strategic expansion in India’s rapidly growing aviation sector.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • Regulatory & Compliance : Advising on compliance with the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA), Ministry of Civil Aviation, Airports Authority of India (AAI), Bureau of Civil Aviation Security, and international aviation regulations.
  • Airport Development & PPP Projects : Assisting in airport development, privatization, concessions, and public-private partnership (PPP) arrangements, including drafting, negotiation, and risk allocation.
  • Aircraft Leasing & Financing : Structuring aircraft and engine leasing, financing, securitization, and sale-and-leaseback transactions, with expertise in Cape Town Convention compliance.
  • Airline Operations : Advising domestic and international airlines on operating permits, route dispersal guidelines, ground handling, code-sharing, alliances, and licensing issues.
  • Commercial & Contractual Advisory : Drafting and negotiating contracts for airport services, fuel supply, MRO, catering, logistics, and airline alliances.
  • Dispute Resolution : Representing clients in disputes involving regulatory actions, contractual claims, liability issues, and arbitrations in aviation and airport-related matters.
  • M&A and Investments : Advising on mergers, acquisitions, private equity investments, and strategic collaborations in airlines, airports, and ancillary aviation services.
  • Aviation Safety & Liability : Guiding clients on liability claims, insurance, consumer disputes, and compliance with safety and security norms.
  • Sustainability & ESG : Supporting clients in addressing carbon emission targets, sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) regulations, and ESG-linked financing in aviation projects.

Why Choose Us?

By combining sectoral insight with multidisciplinary expertise in infrastructure, finance, regulatory law, international trade, and dispute resolution, our Airports and Aviation Practice delivers comprehensive legal solutions tailored to the needs of a globalized aviation market. We are uniquely positioned to help clients navigate India’s growth story in aviation while aligning with international best practices.
