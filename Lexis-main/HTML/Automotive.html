Automotive and Electric Vehicles Practice

India is one of the world’s largest automotive markets and is rapidly transforming into a global hub for electric mobility and advanced automotive technologies. With the Government’s strong policy push through FAME II, Production Linked Incentive (PLI) schemes, and state-level EV policies, along with increasing global investment in sustainable transportation, the automotive and EV sector is undergoing a paradigm shift. This transformation is creating opportunities across manufacturing, R&D, battery technology, charging infrastructure, and mobility-as-a-service, while also presenting unique legal, regulatory, and commercial challenges.

Our Automotive and Electric Vehicles Practice supports multinational manufacturers, suppliers, technology providers, investors, start-ups, and mobility service providers across the automotive and EV value chain. We combine deep sectoral knowledge with cross-disciplinary expertise to deliver comprehensive legal and strategic solutions in this evolving landscape.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • Regulatory & Compliance: Advising on Motor Vehicles Act, CMVR, environmental standards, homologation, safety certifications, and state EV policies, along with global automotive and battery compliance frameworks.
  • Foreign Investment & Market Entry: Assisting global OEMs, EV start-ups, and component manufacturers with foreign direct investment (FDI), joint ventures, strategic alliances, and licensing arrangements in India.
  • Contracts & Commercial Advisory: Drafting and negotiating supply, distribution, dealership, franchising, charging infrastructure agreements, and technology licensing contracts.
  • Electric Mobility & Infrastructure: Advising on charging station projects, battery-swapping models, energy storage solutions, and collaborations between automotive, power, and infrastructure players.
  • Technology & IP: Protecting innovations in connected vehicles, autonomous driving systems, telematics, EV batteries, and charging technologies through patents, trade secrets, and licensing frameworks.
  • M&A and Strategic Transactions: Guiding clients on mergers, acquisitions, private equity, and venture capital investments in automotive and EV companies, including start-up financing.
  • ESG & Sustainability: Advising on green mobility, carbon credits, sustainable supply chains, and compliance with India’s and international ESG norms in the automotive sector.
  • Dispute Resolution: Representing clients in disputes relating to supply contracts, regulatory enforcement, product liability, and cross-border litigation.
  • Competition & Trade: Advising on anti-trust issues, import/export regulations, customs duties, and WTO-linked trade matters affecting automotive and EV components.

Why Choose Us?

With India positioned as both a manufacturing hub and a rapidly expanding consumer market for EVs, our practice is uniquely equipped to bridge the needs of global and domestic stakeholders. By integrating expertise from infrastructure, energy, technology, intellectual property, ESG, and finance practices, we deliver end-to-end legal solutions that help clients capitalize on opportunities in the automotive and electric mobility revolution.
