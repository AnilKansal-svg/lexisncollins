Defence

India has one of the world’s largest defence markets, with increasing focus on indigenization, modernization of armed forces, and private sector participation through the “Make in India” and “Atmanirbhar Bharat” initiatives. The Defence Acquisition Procedure (DAP), liberalized FDI norms, and Production Linked Incentive (PLI) schemes are transforming the sector into a global manufacturing and innovation hub. At the same time, defence projects involve complex regulatory frameworks, sensitive national security considerations, and multi-jurisdictional collaborations.

Our Defence Practice advises multinational defence contractors, Indian manufacturers, technology providers, investors, and start-ups on legal, regulatory, and strategic issues across the defence and aerospace value chain. We combine deep sector knowledge with cross-border expertise to support clients in navigating one of the most highly regulated and strategically important industries.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • Regulatory & Compliance: Advising on compliance with the Defence Acquisition Procedure (DAP), licensing under the Industries (Development & Regulation) Act, FDI policies, offset obligations, and national security regulations.
  • Defence Contracts & Procurement: Assisting in bidding for government tenders, public procurement processes, offset agreements, joint ventures, and transfer-of-technology contracts.
  • M&A and Strategic Transactions: Structuring mergers, acquisitions, foreign direct investment, and joint ventures in defence manufacturing and services.
  • Technology Transfer & Collaborations: Advising on licensing, joint R&D agreements, offset arrangements, and intellectual property rights in defence technologies.
  • Aerospace & Dual-Use Technologies: Supporting clients on aerospace projects, UAVs/drones, cyber defence solutions, and compliance with dual-use export control laws.
  • Dispute Resolution: Representing clients in contract enforcement, regulatory challenges, procurement disputes, and international arbitration.
  • ESG & Sustainability: Guiding on green manufacturing in defence, compliance with ESG-linked financing, and sustainable technology practices.
  • Export Control & Trade Compliance: Advising on international trade restrictions, sanctions, export licensing, and WTO compliance for defence and dual-use goods.
  • Security Clearances & Confidentiality: Assisting clients with security clearance requirements, handling sensitive data, and compliance with classified information protocols.

Why Choose Us?

With India opening its defence sector to greater private and foreign participation, our practice is uniquely positioned to help clients navigate regulatory complexities, structure strategic collaborations, and resolve disputes. By leveraging our expertise across government contracts, trade, ESG, technology, and dispute resolution, we provide comprehensive legal solutions in one of the most sensitive and strategically significant industries.
